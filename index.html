Graduate verification service

To verify the qualification of an ECU graduate, please use this form. If you are unable to use this form, please note that we require written consent from the graduate to disclose information about their course completion. Please ask the student to complete the Consent To Disclose form and attach it in this enquiry form to authorise disclosure of their information. Once the student has provided their consent, please submit your verification request here.

Please note that if you need any further information relating to the qualifications of an ECU alum, please submit an enquiry.
